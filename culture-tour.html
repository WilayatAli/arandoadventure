8-Day Cultural Exploration Gilgit-Baltistan

Tour Name Cost (USD) Cost (EUR)
Cultural Exploration Gilgit-Baltistan (08-Days) - ALL INCLUSIVE US$2,000 €1,826

Description

Join Arando Adventure for an incredible 8-day journey through the enchanting landscapes and vibrant culture of Gilgit-Baltistan. This tour will take you to historic landmarks, lively local communities, and stunning natural wonders. Experience ancient forts, peaceful lakes, and bustling bazaars, all accompanied by traditional music. Dive into the rich heritage and breathtaking beauty of one of Pakistan's most picturesque regions.

HightLights

  • Explore ancient forts such as Baltit and Altit.

  • Visit historic sites like the Chaqchan Mosque and Khaplu Fort.

  • Enjoy scenic drives along the Karakoram Highway and through lush valleys

  • Discover the beauty of Attabad Lake, Borith Lake, and the Passu Glacier.

  • Experience local life in Hunza and Skardu, including traditional folk music and artisan crafts.

  • Stroll along the water channel from Karimabad and cross the Hussani Suspension Bridge.

  • Take an excursion to Khunjerab Pass, the highest border crossing in the world.

Travel Plans

Day-1 : Arrival in Gilgit +

Upon your arrival in Gilgit, you’ll be transferred to your hotel, where you can unwind and recharge after your journey. Take the evening to relax or venture out to explore the local area, soaking in the vibrant atmosphere and beginning to immerse yourself in the unique culture of the region.

Day-2 : Gilgit to Hunza +

Today, you’ll drive from Gilgit to Hunza, covering a distance of 100 kilometers. Along the way, make a stop at Gilgit Bazaar to experience local shopping and culture, then visit the Kargah Buddha, a remarkable ancient carving. You'll also have opportunities to pause at scenic viewpoints for stunning photographs and refreshments. Once you arrive in Hunza, you’ll check into your hotel and settle in for the night.

Day-3 : Hunza Valley Exploration +

Start your day by visiting the historic Baltit Fort, a remarkable structure that's over 700 years old, and explore the surrounding Baltit Village. In the afternoon, delve into the rich history of the 900-year-old Altit Fort and wander through Altit Village. You’ll also have the chance to visit the Women Carpenters Project, where you can see the incredible craftsmanship of local artisans. To wrap up the day, enjoy a peaceful evening walk along the water channel from Karimabad to Hyderabad Village, taking in the beautiful surroundings.

Day-4 : Excursion to Khunjerab Pass +

Today, embark on a day trip to Khunjerab Pass, the highest border crossing between Pakistan and China. Enjoy the breathtaking scenery along the way as you make your journey to this remarkable destination. After exploring the area and taking in the stunning views, you’ll return to Karimabad in the evening, reflecting on the day’s adventures.

Day-5 : Hunza to Skardu +

Today, you’ll embark on a drive from Hunza to Skardu, covering a distance of 290 kilometers. Along the way, take time to stop at various scenic spots, allowing you to soak in the stunning landscapes and capture beautiful photographs. Upon your arrival in Skardu, you’ll check into your hotel and settle in for the night, ready to explore the area in the days to come.

Day-6 : Skardu Exploration +

Today, visit the historic Chaqchan Mosque and Khaplu Fort in Khaplu Village, where you can admire the beautiful architecture and learn about the region's history. Spend some time exploring the local area, including the picturesque nearby villages, which offer a glimpse into the vibrant culture and lifestyle of the residents. In the evening, you’ll make your way back to Skardu, concluding a day filled with exploration and discovery.

Day-7 : Skardu to Chilas +

Today, you’ll drive from Skardu to Chilas, covering a distance of 290 kilometers. After enjoying lunch, you’ll continue your journey with a drive to Besham, which is another 200 kilometers away. Once you arrive in Besham, you’ll check into your hotel and take some time to relax after the long day on the road.

Day-8 : Departure +

Drive back to your departure point or continue your onward journey.
