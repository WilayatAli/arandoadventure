CZECH HOTEL & CAMPGROUND

About Us

Experience comfort and security at our newly built Czech Hotel, offering three cozy rooms, each with a private bathroom and shower. For a unique outdoor adventure, stay in our fenced campground, accommodating up to 20 tents. Enjoy essential amenities like cold-water showers, a common kitchen, and dishwashing facilities. We also provide locally made, authentic meals for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Plus, transportation to and from Skardu is available with our Toyota 4x4, ensuring a seamless stay in this beautiful mountain valley.
