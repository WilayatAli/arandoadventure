3 Days By-Air Tour to Skardu. Heart of GB

Description

Embark on a breathtaking 3-day by-air tour to Skardu, where you'll experience the serene beauty of one of Pakistan's most enchanting destinations. Fly from Islamabad to Skardu and immerse yourself in the natural splendor and cultural richness of the region. This short yet comprehensive tour combines scenic flights, exploration of iconic landmarks, and relaxation in stunning landscapes, ensuring a memorable escape to the heart of the Karakoram Range.

HightLights

  • Scenic Flight: Enjoy a breathtaking aerial view of the majestic peaks of the Karakoram Range as you fly from Islamabad to Skardu.

  • Shangrila Resort: Relax at the picturesque Shangrila Resort, often referred to as "Heaven on Earth," with stunning views of Lower Kachura Lake.

  • Skardu Fort: Explore the historic Skardu Fort, offering panoramic views of the Skardu Valley and the Indus River.

  • Upper Kachura Lake: Visit Upper Kachura Lake, a pristine alpine lake perfect for a serene boat ride and picnic.

  • Deosai National Park: Experience the unique high-altitude plateau of Deosai National Park, known for its diverse wildlife and vast landscapes.

  • Manthal Buddha Rock: Discover ancient rock carvings at Manthal Buddha Rock, reflecting the region's rich cultural history

Travel Plans

1
Day 1: Arrival in Skardu

Morning: Fly from Islamabad to Skardu, enjoying spectacular aerial views of the Karakoram Range.

Afternoon: Arrive in Skardu and transfer to your hotel. Visit the Shangrila Resort, renowned for its stunning location by Lower Kachura Lake.

Evening: Explore the serene surroundings of Shangrila Resort. Enjoy dinner at the hotel with breathtaking views. Overnight stay in Skardu.

2
Day 2: Explore Skardu

Morning: After breakfast, visit Skardu Fort to experience panoramic views of the Skardu Valley and the Indus River. Continue to Upper Kachura Lake for a peaceful boat ride and scenic picnic.

Afternoon: Head to Deosai National Park, often called the "Land of Giants," known for its vast plateau and diverse wildlife. Enjoy a scenic drive and explore the park's unique landscapes.

Evening: Return to Skardu and have dinner at a local restaurant or your hotel. Overnight stay in Skardu.

3
Day 3: Additional Exploration and Departure Preparation

Morning: After breakfast, visit Manthal Buddha Rock to see ancient carvings and inscriptions.

Afternoon: Enjoy some last-minute shopping or relaxation in Skardu. Depending on your interests, you might explore local markets or enjoy a leisurely walk around town.

Evening: Return to your hotel for a farewell dinner, reminiscing about your memorable experiences. Overnight stay in Skardu.

4
Day 4: Return to Islamabad

Morning: Check out from your hotel and transfer to Skardu Airport for your morning flight back to Islamabad.

Package Includes

  • Airfare: Round-trip airfare between Islamabad and Gilgit.

  • Accommodation: Stay in standard hotels with daily breakfast included.

  • Meals: Three meals a day (breakfast, lunch, and dinner). Note: Lunch is optional depending on the specific package.

  • Airport Transfers: Pick-up and drop-off at Gilgit Airport.

  • Ground Transportation: Transportation within Hunza for sightseeing.

  • Guide Services: Professional tour guide services for local excursions.

  • Taxes and Fees: All applicable taxes and airport fees

Package Exclusions

  • Entrance Fees: Costs for entrance to forts and other attractions.

  • Optional Activities: Fees for optional activities and any personal expenses.

  • Travel Insurance: Travel insurance is not included.

  • Tips and Gratuities: Tips for guides, drivers, and other service personnel are not included.
