7-Day Tour: Uncover the Wonders of Hunza & Skardu

Tour Name Cost (USD) Cost (EUR)
Hunza & Skardu (07 days) - ALL INCLUSIVE US$2,000 €1,826

Description

Begin a captivating 7-day journey through the stunning landscapes of Skardu and Hunza during the vibrant autumn months. This thoughtfully crafted itinerary beautifully blends natural beauty, cultural exploration, and unforgettable experiences. Enjoy the tranquility of Shangrila Resort and traverse the majestic Karakoram Highway, all while soaking in the breathtaking views and deep-rooted traditions of Northern Pakistan.

HightLights

  • Karimabad: Immerse yourself in the vibrant local markets and rich culture of Hunza's main town.

  • Baltit and Altit Forts: Explore these historic fortresses for stunning views and deep historical significance.

  • Eagle's Nest: Enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of the Hunza Valley from this high vantage point.

  • Attabad Lake: Marvel at the striking turquoise waters and take a scenic boat ride on this picturesque lake.

  • Khunjerab Pass: Visit the world’s highest paved international border crossing if weather conditions permit.

  • Shangrila Resort: Relax in this beautiful resort, renowned for its stunning landscapes and serene surroundings.

Travel Plans

Day-1 : Arrive at Skardu Airport +

On Day 1, you’ll arrive at Skardu Airport and kick off your journey with a visit to the stunning Shangrila Resort, where you can explore the tranquil Lower Kachura Lake. Afterward, enjoy a peaceful boat ride on Upper Kachura Lake, then venture to the enchanting Xhoq Valley to soak in its breathtaking scenery. Next, discover the unique Katpana Desert and Lake before returning to Skardu city in the evening. To end your day, check into a cozy hotel, ready to relax and recharge for the exciting adventures ahead.

Day-2 : Khaplu Valley and Manthokha Waterfall +

On Day 2, set off on a scenic journey to the beautiful Khaplu Valley, known for its lush landscapes and rich heritage. Start by visiting the historic Khaplu Fort, a 500-year-old structure that provides insight into the region's vibrant past and culture. Next, explore the Chaqchan Mosque, built in 1370 and attributed to Mir Sayyid Ali Hamadani, making it one of the oldest mosques in the area. Continue your adventure to the stunning Manthokha Waterfall, a hidden gem nestled in the heart of Skardu. As you travel, take in the scenic beauty of Kharmang Valley. In the evening, return to your hotel for a delightful dinner and an overnight stay, reflecting on the day’s incredible experiences.

Day-3 : Visit to Shigar Valley +

On Day 3, begin your morning by departing from Skardu and journeying towards the serene Shigar Valley, taking in the breathtaking panoramic views along the route. Arrive at the historic Shigar Fort, an architectural marvel built in the 17th century by the Raja of the Amacha Dynasty. Explore its intricate design and rich history with insights from local guides. Stroll through the local market to experience the vibrant daily life of the valley.

Next, visit the Amburiq Mosque, famous for its beautifully carved wooden architecture, and learn about its significance to the community. Savor a traditional Balti lunch at a local restaurant, enjoying authentic flavors. Afterward, head to Blind Lake in Shigar, followed by a visit to the Sarfaranga Cold Desert, where you can experience the thrill of a jeep safari and even try paragliding over the stunning desert landscape.

Conclude your day with dinner at the hotel and an overnight stay at the Shigar Fort, taking time to reflect on the day's unforgettable adventures.

Day-4 : Skardu to Hunza Valley +

On Day 4, set off early in the morning for Hunza Valley, traveling along the scenic Karakoram Highway. Make a brief stop at Rakaposhi View Point to capture stunning photos of the majestic landscape. Continue your journey with a visit to Altit Fort, a historical site that offers a glimpse into the cultural and architectural heritage of the region. Next, explore Baltit Fort, an ancient fortress providing panoramic views of Hunza Valley. Immerse yourself in the local culture by interacting with friendly residents and shopping for traditional Hunza handicrafts, gems, and souvenirs. As you drive towards Hunza, enjoy the breathtaking beauty of Nagar Valley. In the evening, take in the tranquility of the valley and, if the sky is clear, indulge in some stargazing. Arrive in Hunza by evening and check into your hotel for a relaxing overnight stay.

Day-5 : Toward Attabad Lake, Passu Cone and Suspension Bridge +

On Day 5, begin your day early to maximize your time exploring. Depart from your accommodation in Hunza Valley and enjoy a quick breakfast or pack snacks for the journey. Your first stop will be Attabad Lake, renowned for its stunning turquoise waters. Take a boat ride on the lake to fully appreciate its beauty, and if time permits, explore nearby local villages affected by the lake’s formation in 2010. Continue your scenic drive towards Passu, enjoying the breathtaking views of the Karakoram Range along the way. Once you arrive at the iconic Passu Cones, take in the striking landscape and capture photos of these unique pointed peaks from various vantage points. Enjoy lunch at a local eatery in Passu, sampling traditional Hunza dishes. Next, visit the Hussaini Suspension Bridge, one of the most thrilling and picturesque suspension bridges in the region. Afterward, journey back to Karimabad, where you can have dinner at a local restaurant. Reflect on the day’s adventures and savor the local cuisine as you unwind for the evening.

Day-6 :Hunza to Skardu +

On Day 6, depart from Hunza in the morning, traveling by road along the Karakoram Highway (KKH). This route, while winding, provides awe-inspiring views of the rugged landscape. Make a brief stop at Rakaposhi View Point to capture more stunning photographs. Continue your journey towards Skardu, and visit the Three Mountains Junction, where the Karakoram, Himalaya, and Hindu Kush ranges converge. Next, head to Nanga Parbat View Point to gaze upon the formidable "Killer Mountain." Arrive in Skardu by evening and check into your accommodation for a restful night’s stay

Day-7 : Fly From Skardu to Islmabad or Other Cities +

On Day 7, after enjoying a hearty breakfast, head to Skardu International Airport for your flight. As you conclude your 7-day adventure through Hunza and Skardu, take a moment to reflect on the unforgettable experiences and breathtaking sights. Bid a fond farewell to your driver and tour guide, reluctantly leaving behind the people who made your journey special. Depart with cherished memories of your incredible trip.
