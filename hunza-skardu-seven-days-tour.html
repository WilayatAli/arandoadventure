7 Days Tour: Discover the Wonders of Hunza & Skardu

Description

Set off on a stunning 7-day journey through the mesmerizing landscapes of Skardu and Hunza in the colorful autumn months. This thoughtfully designed itinerary combines natural splendor, cultural discovery, and thrilling experiences. Enjoy the tranquility of Shangrila Resort and traverse the impressive Karakoram Highway, all while soaking in the breathtaking views and deep-rooted traditions of Northern Pakistan.

HightLights

  • Karimabad: Discover the vibrant local markets and immerse yourself in the culture of Karimabad, Hunza's main town.

  • Baltit Fort: Explore the historic Baltit Fort for sweeping views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.

  • Altit Fort: Visit Altit Fort, a culturally significant fortress with deep historical roots.

  • Eagle's Nest: Enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of Hunza Valley from the high vantage point of Eagle's Nest.

  • Rakaposhi Viewpoint: Admire the stunning vistas of Rakaposhi, one of the tallest peaks in the Karakoram Range.

  • Attabad Lake: Marvel at the striking turquoise waters of Attabad Lake and enjoy a scenic boat ride.

  • Passu Cones: Capture memorable photos of the distinctive Passu Cones, a series of dramatic pointed peaks.

  • Hussaini Suspension Bridge: Experience the thrill of crossing the Hussaini Suspension Bridge, known for its picturesque and adventurous allure.

  • Khunjerab Pass: If weather and conditions permit, visit Khunjerab Pass, the world's highest paved international border crossing.

  • Shangrila Resort: Unwind at Shangrila Resort, renowned for its stunning landscapes and the serene Lower Kachura Lake

  • Shigar Fort: Discover Shigar Fort, a historic site with a fascinating mix of Tibetan and Kashmiri architectural styles.

  • Skardu City: Explore Skardu City, wander through local markets, and delve into the region’s vibrant culture.

  • Sheosar Lake: Visit Sheosar Lake, a beautiful glacial lake encircled by lush green meadows.

  • Deosai National Park: Explore Deosai National Park, famed for its unique wildlife, including the Himalayan brown bear.

  • Satpara Lake: Enjoy the tranquil beauty of Satpara Lake, framed by mountains and ideal for boating.

  • Buddha Rock Carvings, Shigar Valley, Khaplu Valley & Monthokha Waterfall.

Travel Plans

1
Arrive at Skardu Airport

On Day 1, you’ll arrive at Skardu Airport and begin your journey with a visit to the picturesque Shangrila Resort, where you can explore the serene Lower Kachura Lake. Afterward, enjoy a relaxing boat ride on Upper Kachura Lake, followed by a trip to the enchanting Xhoq Valley to take in its stunning landscapes. Continue your adventure to the unique Katpana Desert and Lake before heading back to Skardu city in the evening. Conclude your day by checking into a comfortable hotel, ready to rest and recharge for the adventures ahead.

2
Day 2: Khaplu Valley and Manthokha Waterfall

On Day 2, embark on a scenic journey to the picturesque Khaplu Valley, renowned for its lush landscapes and rich heritage. Begin with a visit to the historic Khaplu Fort, a 500-year-old structure that offers a glimpse into the region’s storied past and vibrant local culture. Next, explore the Chaqchan Mosque, dating back to 1370 and attributed to Mir Sayyid Ali Hamadani, making it one of the oldest mosques in the area. Continue your adventure with a visit to the breathtaking Manthokha Waterfall, a hidden gem nestled in the heart of Skardu. Along the way, enjoy the scenic beauty of Kharmang Valley. Return to your hotel for a delightful dinner and an overnight stay, reflecting on the day’s remarkable experiences.

3
Day 3: Visit to Shigar Valley

On Day 3, set out from Skardu in the morning and journey towards the tranquil Shigar Valley, taking in the panoramic views along the way. Arrive at the historic Shigar Fort, an architectural marvel built in the 17th century by the Raja of the Amacha Dynasty. Explore its intricate design and rich history with the help of local guides. Stroll through the local market to experience the vibrant daily life of the valley. Visit the Amburiq Mosque, renowned for its beautifully carved wooden architecture, and learn about its significance in the community. Enjoy a traditional Balti lunch at a local restaurant, savoring the authentic flavors. Continue to Blind Lake in Shigar, followed by a visit to the Sarfaranga Cold Desert. Experience the thrill of a jeep safari and the excitement of paragliding over the desert landscape. Conclude your day with dinner at the hotel and an overnight stay at the Shigar Fort, where you can reflect on the day’s adventures.

4
Day 4: Skardu to Hunza Valley

On Day 4, set off early in the morning for Hunza Valley, traveling along the scenic Karakoram Highway. Make a brief stop at Rakaposhi View Point to capture stunning photos of the majestic landscape. Continue your journey with a visit to Altit Fort, a historical site that offers a glimpse into the cultural and architectural heritage of the region. Next, explore Baltit Fort, an ancient fortress providing panoramic views of Hunza Valley. Immerse yourself in the local culture by interacting with friendly residents and shopping for traditional Hunza handicrafts, gems, and souvenirs. As you drive towards Hunza, enjoy the breathtaking beauty of Nagar Valley. In the evening, take in the tranquility of the valley and, if the sky is clear, indulge in some stargazing. Arrive in Hunza by evening and check into your hotel for a relaxing overnight stay.

5
Day 5: Toward Attabad Lake, Passu Cone and Suspension Bridge

On Day 5, begin your day early to maximize your time exploring. Depart from your accommodation in Hunza Valley and enjoy a quick breakfast or pack snacks for the journey. Your first stop will be Attabad Lake, renowned for its stunning turquoise waters. Take a boat ride on the lake to fully appreciate its beauty, and if time permits, explore nearby local villages affected by the lake’s formation in 2010. Continue your scenic drive towards Passu, enjoying the breathtaking views of the Karakoram Range along the way. Once you arrive at the iconic Passu Cones, take in the striking landscape and capture photos of these unique pointed peaks from various vantage points. Enjoy lunch at a local eatery in Passu, sampling traditional Hunza dishes. Next, visit the Hussaini Suspension Bridge, one of the most thrilling and picturesque suspension bridges in the region. Afterward, journey back to Karimabad, where you can have dinner at a local restaurant. Reflect on the day’s adventures and savor the local cuisine as you unwind for the evening.

6
Day 6: Hunza to Skardu

On Day 6, depart from Hunza in the morning, traveling by road along the Karakoram Highway (KKH). This route, while winding, provides awe-inspiring views of the rugged landscape. Make a brief stop at Rakaposhi View Point to capture more stunning photographs. Continue your journey towards Skardu, and visit the Three Mountains Junction, where the Karakoram, Himalaya, and Hindu Kush ranges converge. Next, head to Nanga Parbat View Point to gaze upon the formidable "Killer Mountain." Arrive in Skardu by evening and check into your accommodation for a restful night’s stay

7
Visit Hopper and Duiker valley

In the morning, embark on an excursion to Hopper Valley, where you can visit the impressive Hopper Glacier and explore the valley's stunning landscapes. In the afternoon, drive for approximately thirty minutes to Duiker Valley and check into your hotel. Duiker Valley offers breathtaking viewpoints for watching both the sunrise and sunset over the majestic mountains surrounding Hunza.

8
Fly From Skardu to Islmabad or Other Cities

On Day 7, after enjoying a hearty breakfast, head to Skardu International Airport for your flight. As you conclude your 7-day adventure through Hunza and Skardu, take a moment to reflect on the unforgettable experiences and breathtaking sights. Bid a fond farewell to your driver and tour guide, reluctantly leaving behind the people who made your journey special. Depart with cherished memories of your incredible trip.
