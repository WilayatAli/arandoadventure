Broad Peak Expedition: Conquer the 8,047m Summit

Description

Broad Peak, originally referred to as K3, is the 12th highest mountain in the world, situated along the China-Pakistan border. It forms part of the Gasherbrum massif and lies approximately 5 miles from K2. The name "Broad Peak" was adopted after it was found that the summit extended over a mile in length, making it distinct from the initially assigned name. Locally, it is known as Faichan Kangri.

The mountain was first successfully climbed on June 9, 1957, by an Austrian team led by Marcus Schmuck, which included Fritz Wintersteller, Kurt Diemberger, and Hermann Buhl. Their initial attempt on May 29 saw Wintersteller and Diemberger reach the forepeak at 8,030 meters. Remarkably, they achieved this ascent without supplemental oxygen, high-altitude porters, or support from a base camp.

Travel Plans

1
Day 01: Arrive Islamabad, transfer to hotel .
2
Day 02: Briefing at Ministry of Tourism. Meeting with LO (Liaison Officer).
3
Day 03: Fly to Skardu (1 hr) or drive to Chilas.
4
Day 04: Day free at Skardu or drive Chilas – Skardu (8-10 hrs).
5
Day 05: Free Day at Skardu for final preparation
6
Day 06: Skardu – Askole (6-7 hrs)
7
Day 07: Trek to Jhula near Domardo River. (8-9 hours)
8
Day 08: Trek to Paiyu provides good views of Baltoro South, Cathedral Towers and triangle of K2 at far view (5-7 hrs)
9
Day 09: Day free at Paiyu for acclimatization (3600 m)
10
Day 10: Trek to Urdukus: To Khuburtze (8-9 hrs): A gradual climb up Baltoro Glacier on The southern edge. The walk mostly along the crest of lateral moraine, with stunning views of Paiyu Peak, Chorichi, Uli Biaho, Trango Towers and so on. A good camp site at 4200 m.
11
Day 11: Goro II: The first hour walk is across difficult side crevasses; however the way is smoother and easy in the center. Continue opposite of Yermanendo Glacier for 3-4 hrs to the camp site at 4500 meters pitched in the center of the glacier.
12
Day 12: Concordia: 5-6 hours slightly easy walk, with spectacular views and gigantic mountain panorama, including Mustagh Tower, GB IV, Mitre Peak and the mighty K2. Camp at Concordia at 4700 m.
13
Day 13: 2-3 hours trek to Broad Peak base camp
14
Day 14-37: Climb Broad Peak. No. of days depends on weather, fitness, and experience of the climbers.
15
Day 38-43: Return to Askole or Hushe and drive to Skardu. In case of trek via same route
16
Day 42: Fly to Islamabad or drive to Chilas
17
Day 43: Day free at Islamabad or drive to Islamabad
18
Day 44: Fly to destination.
