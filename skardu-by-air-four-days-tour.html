Description

Discover the enchanting Skardu Valley, where rich culture meets stunning natural beauty for an unforgettable experience. This tour takes you through Pakistan’s cultural and natural treasures, starting with the ancient Skardu Fort, which reflects the region’s storied history. Stroll through the vibrant Shigar Bazaar, where traditional crafts and local life come to life. The Amburiq Mosque, dating back to 1370, features exquisite wooden craftsmanship and a sense of spiritual tranquility. Immerse yourself in the warmth of Balti hospitality, savoring traditional cuisine and engaging with the timeless rituals and music that highlight the region's rich heritage. Skardu Valley itself is a breathtaking masterpiece, showcasing the serene Lower Kachura Lake and the lush landscapes of Shigar Valley, all set against the majestic backdrop of Deosai National Park. Here, every view reveals towering mountains, crystal-clear rivers, and a panorama that truly captivates the soul.

HightLights

  • Discover the ancient Skardu Fort and delve into the region’s rich history. Stroll through Shigar Bazaar, a vibrant marketplace showcasing local culture.

  • Admire the architectural beauty of the Amburiq Mosque.

  • Savor traditional Balti cuisine and experience the region’s warm hospitality.

  • Marvel at Lower Kachura Lake and Shigar Valley’s lush landscapes. Explore Deosai National Park, a haven for wildlife enthusiasts.

Travel Plans

Day-1 : Arrival in Skardu +

Upon arriving in Skardu via a scenic flight from Islamabad with PIA or Air Blue, you’ll be welcomed by our guide at the airport. Your exploration begins at Shangrila Resort, famously known as "Heaven on Earth," where you can capture its beauty through photography. Next, enjoy the tranquility of Lower Kachura Lake, followed by a delightful boat ride on Upper Kachura Lake. Continue your journey to the picturesque Xhoq Valley before heading into Skardu city. Conclude your day with dinner at your hotel and settle in for a restful overnight stay.

Day-2 : Khaplu Valley and Manthokha Waterfall +

Set off on a journey to the enchanting Khaplu Valley, celebrated for its lush landscapes. Visit the historic Khaplu Fort, a testament to the region's rich heritage, and explore the ancient Chaqchan Mosque, dating back to 1370 and attributed to Mir Sayyid Ali Hamadani. Discover the awe-inspiring Manthokha Waterfall, a hidden gem nestled in Skardu, and take in the stunning views of Kharmang Valley along the way. Return to Skardu by evening, enjoy dinner at your hotel, and unwind for the night at your accommodation.

Day-3 : Visit to Shigar Valley +

Begin your day with a drive from Skardu to Shigar Valley, soaking in the panoramic views along the way. Visit the historic Shigar Fort, built in the 17th century by the Raja of the Amacha Dynasty, and delve into its rich history. Wander through local markets to experience the essence of daily life. Visit the Amburiq Mosque, renowned for its beautifully carved wooden structure. Enjoy a traditional Balti lunch at a local restaurant, and then visit Blind Lake. Proceed to the Sarfaranga Cold Desert for a jeep safari and optional paragliding experience. Return to Skardu, enjoy dinner at your hotel, and stay overnight.

Day-4 : Return to Islamabad +

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and head to the airport. Fly back to Islamabad, cherishing the memories of an unforgettable expedition through the captivating landscapes and rich culture of Skardu Valley.
