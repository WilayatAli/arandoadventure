8 Days Spring Serenity: Explore the Blossoming Beauty of Hunza and Skardu

Description

Explore the majestic Hunza Valley and the enchanting Fairy Meadows on this immersive 7-day journey. This tour offers a perfect blend of breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural experiences, and thrilling adventures.

HightLights

  • Islamabad to Chilas: Travel along the iconic Karakoram Highway, enjoying picturesque landscapes and scenic beauty.

  • Chilas to Raikot Bridge: Traverse through stunning terrain to reach the gateway of Fairy Meadows.

  • Fairy Meadows Trek: Trek through lush landscapes to the serene Fairy Meadows campsite, with views of Nanga Parbat.

  • Hunza Valley Exploration: Discover the wonders of Hunza, including Karimabad, Baltit Fort, and Attabad Lake

  • Passu Cones and Hussaini Suspension Bridge: Experience the iconic Passu Cones and the thrilling Hussaini Suspension Bridge.

Travel Plans

1
Day 1: Islamabad to Chilas

Morning: Depart from Islamabad early in the morning. Travel along the Karakoram Highway (KKH), taking in the scenic beauty of Abbottabad and Besham.
Afternoon: Arrive in Chilas and check in at your hotel. Enjoy dinner with views of the surrounding mountains.

2
Day 2: Chilas to Raikot Bridge and Fairy Meadows

Morning: After breakfast, continue your journey towards Raikot Bridge. Transfer to local jeeps for a thrilling ride to Tatu Village, the starting point of your trek.
Afternoon: Trek from Tatu Village to Fairy Meadows. Set up camp and take in the stunning views of Nanga Parbat.
Evening: Enjoy dinner at the campsite amidst the breathtaking natural beauty of Fairy Meadows.

3
Day 3: Fairy Meadows Exploration

Full Day: Explore Fairy Meadows at your leisure. Optional hike to the Nanga Parbat Base Camp for an up-close view of the world’s ninth-highest peak. Engage with locals and capture stunning photographs.
Evening: Return to your campsite for dinner and an overnight stay.

4
Day 4: Fairy Meadows to Hunza Valley

Morning: Trek back to Tatu Village and transfer to Raikot Bridge. Drive towards Hunza Valley, enjoying the scenic drive through the Karakoram Highway.
Afternoon: Arrive in Hunza and check in at your hotel. Relax and take in the panoramic views.
Evening: Dinner at a local restaurant and overnight stay in Hunza.

5
Day 5: Exploring Hunza Valley

Morning: Visit Karimabad and explore the local bazaars. Tour Baltit Fort for panoramic views and historical insights.
Afternoon: Explore Altit Fort, known for its rich cultural heritage. Visit Attabad Lake to enjoy its turquoise waters and take a boat ride.
Evening: Return to your hotel for dinner and an overnight stay in Hunza.

6
Day 6: Passu Cones and Hussaini Suspension Bridge

Morning: Depart for Passu. Admire the iconic Passu Cones, a group of pointed peaks perfect for photographs.
Afternoon: Head to the Hussaini Suspension Bridge, known for its thrilling and picturesque setting. Experience the bridge and enjoy the surrounding views.
Evening: Return to Hunza, have dinner at a local restaurant, and reflect on the day’s adventures.

7
Day 7: Hunza to Islamabad

Morning: Depart from Hunza early and travel back towards Islamabad, enjoying the scenic drive along the Karakoram Highway.
Afternoon: Arrive in Islamabad and check in at your hotel if needed. Spend some free time exploring the city or relaxing.
Evening: Conclude your tour with a farewell dinner and reminisce about your memorable journey.
