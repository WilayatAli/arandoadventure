9-Day Autumn Escape: Uncover the Enchantment of Hunza and Skardu

Tour Name Cost (USD) Cost (EUR)
Autumn Tour Skardu & Hunza (09 days) - ALL INCLUSIVE US$2,000 €1,826

Description

Set out on a captivating 8-day adventure through the stunning landscapes of Hunza and Skardu during the enchanting autumn season. As the leaves turn golden and the crisp air settles in, you’ll be surrounded by vibrant colors and the natural beauty of these regions. This tour beautifully combines cultural exploration, scenic drives, and breathtaking natural wonders, offering a comprehensive insight into the heart of Gilgit-Baltistan.

HightLights

  • Historic Forts: Explore the iconic Baltit and Altit Forts in Hunza.

  • Autumn Foliage: Enjoy stunning autumn colors and panoramic views from various viewpoints.

  • Serene Lakes: Visit the tranquil Attabad Lake and picturesque Borith Lake.

  • Majestic Glaciers: Discover the breathtaking beauty of Passu Glacier and cross the Hussaini Suspension Bridge.

  • Local Culture: Experience traditional folk music and artisan projects to immerse yourself in the local culture.

  • Scenic Drive: Take a scenic journey along the Karakoram Highway and admire the region's rugged beauty.

  • Khunjerab Pass: Venture to the spectacular Khunjerab Pass, the highest border crossing in the world.

Travel Plans

Day-1 : Pick up From Islamabd / Rawalpindi +

Set off on an incredible 11-12 hour drive to Chilas, covering 461 kilometers along the legendary Karakoram Highway. This historic route connects the Gilgit-Baltistan region to the ancient Silk Road, stretching about 1,300 kilometers (810 miles) from Kashgar in China’s Xinjiang region to Abbottabad and Hassan Abdal in Pakistan. As you navigate this breathtaking highway, you'll encounter some of the world's most stunning landscapes, featuring towering peaks, deep valleys, and rugged terrains, all while immersing yourself in the rich history and vibrant cultural tapestry of this extraordinary corridor.

Day-2 : Drive from Chilas to Skardu +

On Day 2, enjoy an enchanting drive from Chilas to Skardu, covering approximately 290 kilometers in about 8-10 hours. This scenic route winds through dramatic mountain landscapes and alongside the Indus River, providing breathtaking views of the Karakoram range. As you travel, you'll pass through charming villages, immersing yourself in the region's rugged beauty. Take a mid-day break for lunch at a local eatery before arriving in Skardu by late afternoon or early evening. After checking into your accommodation, indulge in a delightful dinner featuring traditional Balti cuisine, then unwind in preparation for the exciting adventures that await you.

Day-3 : Gilgit to Hunza +

Set off on a drive from Gilgit to Hunza, covering a distance of 100 kilometers. Along the way, you'll visit the bustling Gilgit Bazaar and the historic Kargah Buddha. Be sure to stop at various viewpoints for photography and refreshments, taking in the breathtaking scenery. Upon arrival in Hunza, check into your hotel and settle in for your stay.

Day-4 : Hunza Valley Exploration +

Begin your day by visiting the 700-year-old Baltit Fort and exploring the charming Baltit Village. In the afternoon, head to the 900-year-old Altit Fort and take a stroll through Altit Village, immersing yourself in the local history and culture. Visit the Women Carpenters Project to appreciate the craftsmanship of local artisans. As the day winds down, enjoy a peaceful evening walk along the water channel from Karimabad to Hyderabad Village, soaking in the serene surroundings.

Day-5 : Excursion to Khunjerab Pass +

Take a day trip to Khunjerab Pass, the highest border crossing between Pakistan and China. Enjoy the stunning scenery along the way and immerse yourself in the unique atmosphere of this remarkable location. After exploring the area and taking in the breathtaking views, you'll return to Karimabad in the evening, reflecting on the day’s memorable adventure.

Day-6 :Hunza to Skardu +

Set off on your drive from Hunza to Skardu, covering a distance of 290 kilometers. Along the way, enjoy the breathtaking landscapes and charming villages that dot the route. As you travel, take in the stunning views and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the region. Upon your arrival in Skardu, check into your hotel and settle in for the night, ready for the adventures that lie ahead.

Day-7 : Skardu Exploration +

Visit the historic Chaqchan Mosque and Khaplu Fort in Khaplu Village, where you can admire the beautiful architecture and learn about the area's rich history. Take some time to explore the local surroundings and enjoy the stunning natural beauty of the region. In the evening, return to Skardu, reflecting on the day's enriching experiences.

Day-8 : Skardu to Chilas +

Set out on your drive from Skardu to Chilas, covering a distance of 290 kilometers. After enjoying lunch, continue your journey with an additional 200 kilometers to Besham. Upon your arrival in Besham, check into your hotel and take some time to relax after the day's travels.

Day-9 : Departure +

Drive back to your departure point or continue your onward journey.
