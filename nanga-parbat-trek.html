Nanga Parbat Circular Trek, Pakistan

Description

Explore the stunning landscapes around Nanga Parbat on this 15-day trek in northern Pakistan. This challenging trek takes you through remote mountain villages, alpine meadows, dense forests, and across glaciers, all with breathtaking views of Nanga Parbat, the 9th highest mountain in the world. Along the way, you'll experience the beauty of the Himalayas and the warmth of the local people.

HightLights

  • Trek to Nanga Parbat Base Camp, known as the "Killer Mountain"

  • Cross the high-altitude Mazeno Pass (5,399 meters)

  • Stunning views of the Rupal and Diamir faces of Nanga Parbat

  • Experience Fairy Meadows, famous for its beauty

  • Hike through diverse landscapes: forests, glaciers, and alpine meadows

  • Stay in local villages and learn about traditional cultures

  • Camp under the stars with panoramic mountain views

  • Encounter wildlife such as ibex, snow leopards, and Himalayan bears

Travel Plans

1
Day 1: Arrival in Islamabad

Arrive in Islamabad and transfer to your hotel.

Meet your guide and get a briefing about the trek..

2
Day 2: Islamabad to Chilas

Drive along the scenic Karakoram Highway to Chilas.

Overnight at a hotel in Chilas.

3
Day 3: Chilas to Fairy Meadows

Drive to Raikot Bridge and take a jeep to Tattu Village.

Start trekking to Fairy Meadows and set up camp.

4
Day 4: Acclimatization at Fairy Meadows

Rest day to explore Fairy Meadows and acclimatize.

Enjoy incredible views of Nanga Parbat.

5
Day 5: Fairy Meadows to Beyal Camp

Trek through forests to Beyal Camp.

Camp overnight with views of Nanga Parbat.

6
Day 6: Beyal Camp to Nanga Parbat Base Camp

Hike to Nanga Parbat Base Camp (Rupal face).

Spend the night at camp near the base of the mountain.

7
Day 7: Nanga Parbat Base Camp to Mazeno Base Camp

Trek to Mazeno Base Camp, passing beautiful valleys and meadows.

Camp at Mazeno Base..

8
Cross Mazeno Pass

Cross the challenging Mazeno Pass (5,399 meters).

Descend into Diamir Valley and camp.

9
Day 9: Mazeno to Diamir Base Camp

Trek to the Diamir Base Camp on the other side of Nanga Parbat.

Camp overnight.

9
Day 9: Mazeno to Diamir Base Camp

Trek to the Diamir Base Camp on the other side of Nanga Parbat.

Camp overnight.

10
Day 7: Nanga Parbat Base Camp to Mazeno Base Camp

Trek to Mazeno Base Camp, passing beautiful valleys and meadows.

Camp at Mazeno Base..

11
Cross Mazeno Pass

Cross the challenging Mazeno Pass (5,399 meters).

Descend into Diamir Valley and camp.

12
Day 9: Mazeno to Diamir Base Camp

Trek to the Diamir Base Camp on the other side of Nanga Parbat.

Camp overnight.

13
Day 9: Mazeno to Diamir Base Camp

Trek to the Diamir Base Camp on the other side of Nanga Parbat.

Camp overnight.
