8 Days Cultural Exploration: Immerse in the Rich Heritage of Gilgit-Baltistan

Description

Experience the rich cultural tapestry and stunning landscapes of Gilgit-Baltistan on this 8-day tour. This journey takes you through historic sites, vibrant local communities, and breathtaking natural wonders, offering a deep dive into the region's heritage and beauty. From ancient forts and serene lakes to vibrant bazaars and traditional music, this tour provides an immersive experience in one of Pakistan's most picturesque and culturally rich areas.

HightLights

  • Explore ancient forts like Baltit and Altit.

  • Visit the historic Chaqchan Mosque and Khaplu Fort.

  • Enjoy scenic drives along the Karakoram Highway and through lush valleys.

  • Discover the beauty of Attabad Lake, Borith Lake, and the Passu Glacier.

  • Experience local life in Hunza and Skardu, including traditional folk music and artisan projects.

  • Walk along the water channel from Karimabad and cross the Hussani Suspension Bridge.

  • Take an excursion to Khunjerab Pass, the highest border crossing in the world.

Travel Plans

1
Day 1: Arrival in Gilgit

Arrive in Gilgit.

Transfer to your hotel and relax after your journey.

Evening at leisure or explore the local area.

2
Gilgit to Hunza

Drive from Gilgit to Hunza (100 km).

En-route, visit Gilgit Bazaar and Kargah Buddha.

Stop at various viewpoints for photography and refreshments.

Arrive in Hunza, check into your hotel.

3
Day 3: Hunza Valley Exploration

Visit the 700-year-old Baltit Fort and Baltit Village.

In the afternoon, explore the 900-year-old Altit Fort and Altit Village.

Visit the Women Carpenters Project.

Enjoy an evening walk along the water channel from Karimabad to Hyderabad Village.

4
Day 4: Excursion to Khunjerab Pass

Take a day trip to Khunjerab Pass, the Pakistan-China border.

Return to Karimabad in the evening.

5
Day 5: Hunza to Skardu

Drive from Hunza to Skardu (290 km).

En-route, visit scenic spots along the way.

Arrive in Skardu and check into your hotel.

6
Day 6: Skardu Exploration

Visit the historic Chaqchan Mosque and Khaplu Fort in Khaplu Village.

Explore the local area, including the scenic villages.

Drive back to Skardu in the evening.

7
Day 7: Skardu to Chilas

Drive from Skardu to Chilas (290 km).

After lunch, continue the drive to Besham (200 km).

Arrive in Besham and check into your hotel.

8
Day 8: Departure

Drive back to your departure point or continue your onward journey.
