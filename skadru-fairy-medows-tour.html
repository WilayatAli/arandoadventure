8-Day Adventure: Discover Skardu and Fairy Meadows

Tour Name Cost (USD) Cost (EUR)
Skardu and Fairy Meadows (08 days) - ALL INCLUSIVE US$2,000 €1,826

Description

Set off on an exhilarating 8-day adventure through the breathtaking landscapes of Skardu and Fairy Meadows. This journey takes you from the tranquil beauty of Skardu to the awe-inspiring vistas of Fairy Meadows, combining natural wonders, scenic treks, and rich cultural experiences. Explore the majesty of towering peaks, serene lakes, and lush meadows while immersing yourself in the vibrant local culture and warm hospitality. This tour promises unforgettable memories and a deep connection with the stunning surroundings.

HightLights

  • Skardu Fort and Sheosar Lake: Explore the historic Skardu Fort and enjoy the serene beauty of Sheosar Lake.

  • Fairy Meadows Trek: Embark on a trek to the enchanting Fairy Meadows, offering stunning views of Nanga Parbat.

  • Shangrila Resort: Visit the picturesque Shangrila Resort at Lower Kachura Lake and explore the scenic Upper Kachura Lake.

  • Local Culture: Experience the rich culture and warm hospitality of Skardu and its surrounding villages.

  • Karakoram Mountain Range: Enjoy breathtaking views of the majestic Karakoram mountain range throughout your journey.

  • Karakoram Highway Drive: Take a scenic drive along the iconic Karakoram Highway, surrounded by picturesque landscapes.

Travel Plans

Day-1 : Pick up From Islamabd / Rawalpindi +

Set off on an incredible 11-12 hour drive to Chilas, covering 461 kilometers along the legendary Karakoram Highway. This historic route connects the Gilgit-Baltistan region to the ancient Silk Road, stretching about 1,300 kilometers (810 miles) from Kashgar in China’s Xinjiang region to Abbottabad and Hassan Abdal in Pakistan. As you navigate this breathtaking highway, you'll encounter some of the world's most stunning landscapes, featuring towering peaks, deep valleys, and rugged terrains, all while immersing yourself in the rich history and vibrant cultural tapestry of this extraordinary corridor.

Day-2 : Explore Skardu +

Visit Skardu Fort to explore its historical significance and enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding valley. Afterward, discover the beauty of Lower Kachura Lake at Shangrila Resort and Upper Kachura Lake, where you can soak in the serene landscapes. Conclude your day by returning to your hotel, allowing yourself some time to relax and reflect on the day's adventures.

Day-3 : Drive to Fairy Meadows +

Drive from Skardu to Raikot Bridge, which takes approximately 2-3 hours. Once there, cross the Indus River by jeep and begin your trek to Fairy Meadows, a journey that will take around 3-4 hours. Upon arrival at Fairy Meadows, check into your campsite or lodge, ready to immerse yourself in the stunning natural beauty of the area.

Day-4 : Explore Fairy Meadows +

Enjoy a full day in Fairy Meadows, where you can soak in the stunning views of Nanga Parbat, the world’s ninth-highest peak. Take advantage of optional short hikes to explore the surrounding area, or simply relax and immerse yourself in the majestic scenery, breathing in the fresh mountain air.

Day-5 : Trek to Nanga Parbat Base Camp (Optional) +

Embark on an optional trek to Nanga Parbat Base Camp for a closer view of the towering peak, depending on weather conditions and your fitness level. After this unforgettable experience, return to Fairy Meadows for the night, where you can relax and reflect on your adventure.

Day-6 :Return to Skardu +

Trek back to Raikot Bridge and then drive to Skardu. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and take some time to relax after the journey, recharging for the next day’s adventures.

Day-7 : Explore Skardu and Surroundings +

Spend the day visiting any missed attractions or exploring local markets for souvenirs. Enjoy a leisurely experience as you immerse yourself in the local culture, or take a short excursion to nearby spots, allowing for a deeper connection with the vibrant surroundings.

Day-8 : Departure +

Drive back to your departure point or continue your onward journey.
