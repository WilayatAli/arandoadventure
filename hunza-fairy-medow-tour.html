7-Day Journey: Unveil the Beauty of Hunza and Fairy Meadows

Hunza and Fairy Meadows (07 days) - ALL INCLUSIVE US$2,000 €1,826

Description

Join us for a breathtaking 7-day adventure through the stunning landscapes of Hunza and Fairy Meadows. Experience the rich heritage of Hunza with visits to iconic Baltit and Altit Forts, and immerse yourself in the local culture as you explore charming villages. Then, journey to Fairy Meadows, nestled at the base of Nanga Parbat, where you can trek through lush meadows and soak in awe-inspiring mountain views. Enjoy scenic drives along the Karakoram Highway, capturing picturesque vistas and the vibrant colors of nature. This tour promises an unforgettable blend of natural beauty and cultural richness.

HightLights

  • Islamabad to Chilas: Journey along the iconic Karakoram Highway, taking in picturesque landscapes and stunning scenery.

  • Chilas to Raikot Bridge: Traverse breathtaking terrain as you approach the gateway to Fairy Meadows.

  • Fairy Meadows Trek: Hike through lush landscapes to the tranquil Fairy Meadows campsite, where you'll enjoy magnificent views of Nanga Parbat.

  • Hunza Valley Exploration: Discover the wonders of Hunza, including the charming town of Karimabad, the historic Baltit Fort, and the serene Attabad Lake.

  • Passu Cones and Hussaini Suspension Bridge: Experience the striking Passu Cones and the exhilarating Hussaini Suspension Bridge for an unforgettable adventure.

Travel Plans

Day-1 : Pick up From Islamabd / Rawalpindi +

Set off on an incredible 11-12 hour drive to Chilas, covering 461 kilometers along the legendary Karakoram Highway. This historic route connects the Gilgit-Baltistan region to the ancient Silk Road, stretching about 1,300 kilometers (810 miles) from Kashgar in China’s Xinjiang region to Abbottabad and Hassan Abdal in Pakistan. As you navigate this breathtaking highway, you'll encounter some of the world's most stunning landscapes, featuring towering peaks, deep valleys, and rugged terrains, all while immersing yourself in the rich history and vibrant cultural tapestry of this extraordinary corridor.

Day-2 : Chilas to Raikot Bridge and Fairy Meadows +

After breakfast, continue your journey toward Raikot Bridge and transfer to local jeeps for a thrilling ride to Tatu Village, the starting point of your trek. In the afternoon, trek from Tatu Village to Fairy Meadows, where you’ll set up camp and soak in the stunning views of Nanga Parbat. As evening falls, enjoy dinner at the campsite, surrounded by the breathtaking natural beauty of Fairy Meadows.

Day-3 : Fairy Meadows Exploration +

Spend the full day exploring Fairy Meadows at your leisure. You have the option to hike to Nanga Parbat Base Camp for an up-close view of the world’s ninth-highest peak. Take time to engage with the friendly locals and capture stunning photographs of the breathtaking scenery. In the evening, return to your campsite for a delightful dinner and an overnight stay, surrounded by the beauty of the mountains.

Day-4 : Fairy Meadows to Hunza Valley +

In the morning, trek back to Tatu Village and transfer to Raikot Bridge before driving toward Hunza Valley, enjoying the scenic route along the Karakoram Highway. In the afternoon, arrive in Hunza and check into your hotel, where you can relax and take in the panoramic views of the stunning landscape. In the evening, savor dinner at a local restaurant, followed by an overnight stay in Hunza, surrounded by its enchanting beauty.

Day-5 : Exploring Hunza Valley +

In the morning, visit Karimabad to explore the vibrant local bazaars and then tour Baltit Fort, where you can enjoy panoramic views and learn about its historical significance. In the afternoon, continue to Altit Fort, celebrated for its rich cultural heritage, before heading to Attabad Lake to admire its striking turquoise waters and take a scenic boat ride. In the evening, return to your hotel for dinner and an overnight stay in Hunza, soaking in the enchanting surroundings

Day-6 :Passu Cones and Hussaini Suspension Bridge +

In the morning, depart for Passu and admire the iconic Passu Cones, a stunning group of pointed peaks that provide perfect photo opportunities. In the afternoon, head to the Hussaini Suspension Bridge, renowned for its thrilling experience and picturesque setting. Take your time to cross the bridge and soak in the breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. In the evening, return to Hunza, enjoy dinner at a local restaurant, and reflect on the day’s adventures amidst the beauty of the region.

Day-7 : Hunza to Islamabad +

In the morning, depart from Hunza early and travel back toward Islamabad, enjoying the scenic drive along the Karakoram Highway. In the afternoon, arrive in Islamabad and check into your hotel if needed, giving yourself some free time to explore the city or relax. In the evening, conclude your tour with a farewell dinner, taking the opportunity to reminisce about the memorable experiences and breathtaking sights from your journey.
