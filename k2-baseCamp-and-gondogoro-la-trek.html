Description

Embark on a mesmerizing 20-day journey through the rugged Karakoram Range with arando Adventures' Gondogoro La Pass Trek. This unforgettable odyssey leads you through breathtaking landscapes, from the road journey to Askole and the enchanting Braldu River to the stunning Baltoro Glacier and the panoramic marvel of Concordia, where K2 and the Gasherbrum range dominate the skyline. Experience the thrill of summiting the high-altitude Gondogoro La Pass at 5,940 meters, and enjoy a new perspective as you descend into the charming Hushe Valley. The trek concludes with a scenic journey back to Skardu and a comfortable return flight to Islamabad, leaving you with lasting memories of an extraordinary adventure.

HightLights

  • Arrival in Islamabad: Begin your adventure with a warm welcome at Islamabad International Airport, followed by a briefing and overnight stay in the city.

  • Scenic Flight to Skardu: Enjoy breathtaking aerial views of Nanga Parbat and the Himalayan peaks on your flight to Skardu, or opt for a scenic road journey if flights are unavailable.

  • Pre-Trek Exploration in Skardu: Take a day to prepare for the trek, with a final gear check and exploration of Skardu's vibrant bazaars.

  • Journey to Askole: Drive through rugged terrain to Askole, the last village before the wilderness, and start your trek with an overnight camp.

  • Trek Through the Braldu River Gorge: Experience picturesque landscapes as you trek along the Braldu River, crossing wooden bridges and setting up camp in Jhula and Paiju.

  • Concordia – The Throne Room of the Gods: Reach the heart of the Karakoram at Concordia, where you’ll be surrounded by iconic peaks including K2, Broad Peak, and the Gasherbrum range.

  • Summit Gondogoro La Pass: Challenge yourself with the ascent of Gondogoro La Pass (5,940m) and enjoy unparalleled views of the Karakoram range before descending to Khuspang.

  • Return to Civilization: After descending to Hushe, travel back through the scenic Shyok and Indus river valleys to Skardu, and enjoy the comforts of a hotel before your return flight to Islamabad.

Travel Plans

Day-1 : Arrive at Islamabad International Airport, Islamabad +

After pickup, you’ll be transferred to our partner hotel to relax and recover from your journey.

Day-1 : Arrive at Islamabad International Airport, Islamabad +

After pickup, you’ll be transferred to our partner hotel to relax and recover from your journey.

Day-2 : Fly to Skardu +

Participants traveling this morning will enjoy a stunning flight from Islamabad to Skardu, with a chance to see Nanga Parbat, weather permitting. Upon arrival in Skardu, the day is yours to explore at your leisure.

Day-3 : Spend free day in Skardu +

After breakfast, participants will hike to Kharpocho Fort, perched on a mountain with breathtaking views of the Indus River and Skardu town. You’ll also have the option to visit Sadpara Lake, followed by free time for shopping in Skardu Bazaar. While you enjoy the sights, our staff will handle the necessary government documentation, which may take up to two days. We’ve built in contingency days at the end of the trek to accommodate any delays.

Day-4 : Drive to Askole village +

Participants will embark on a jeep safari to Daso, traveling through the stunning Shigar Valley alongside the Braldu River, which extends to Askole. Askole marks the last village on our route, where participants will start their trek after leaving the jeeps behind. Depending on road conditions, you may begin your walk before reaching Askole. The journey will take approximately six to eight hours to complete.

Day-5 : Trek from Askole to Jhola via the snout of Biafo Glacier +

After finalizing the necessary supplies and equipment, the porters will prepare for the trek to Jhola. This valley serves as the gateway to the Biafo Glacier, which flows over fifty kilometers from Hispar La to Braldu. Participants will navigate the moraine as they cross the glacier's snout and descend to the lunch spot beside the Korofon River.

The route will lead us to the confluence of the Braldu and Domordo rivers. Instead of the older trail, we’ll take a new path upstream, crossing the river via a bridge. After crossing, participants will descend to the edge of the Braldu River and follow the riverbank to reach the camp at Jhola.

Day-6 : Trek from Jhola to Paiju following the Braldu river +

On this day of the K2 Base Camp trek, participants will follow the Braldu Gorge along the riverbank, enjoying stunning views of Trango Tower and Cathedral Peak. To avoid sunburn, it's important to stay protected, as conditions can be dry and hot.

Along the way, several glacier-fed streams may require river crossing shoes, depending on water levels. On a clear day, you’ll spot the snout of Baltoro Glacier. The day will conclude with a climb away from the river to reach the camp at Paiju, the last campsite with trees for a while.

Day-7 : Acclimatize and rest in Paiju +

Participants will spend the day at Paiju Camp for acclimatization and rest. Meanwhile, the porters will bake bread for the upcoming week on the glacier. For those interested, an acclimatization walk to the base camp of Paiju Peak is also available.

Day-8 : Trek from Paiju to Khoburtse +

On this day, the trek will be challenging, but the scenery will become increasingly breathtaking as you approach the snout of Baltoro Glacier. The trail features continuous up and down climbs over the glacial moraine; while it's generally safe, participants should remain cautious at all times.

As you progress, you'll enjoy stunning views of Trango Tower and Uli Biaho. Participants will need to cross the glacier to reach Liligo, after which the route will follow a rocky trail along the edge of the Baltoro Glacier, eventually leading to Khoburtse, where camp will be set up.

Day-9 : Khuburse to Urdukas +

Ascend towards Urdukas, offering panoramic views of peaks like Masherbrum. Camp at Urdukas for breathtaking vistas.

Day-10 : Trek to Goro II, the junction of the Baltoro glaciers +

Hike along the Baltoro Glacier to Goro II, a high-altitude camp with spectacular views of the glacier and peaks such as Gasherbrum IV.

Day-11 : Trek to Concordia through a rocky moraine +

Traverse the Baltoro Glacier to Concordia, the heart of the Karakoram. Camp here and marvel at the stunning views of K2, Broad Peak, and Gasherbrum I and II.

Day-12 : Concordia (Exploration Day) +

Explore Concordia, known as the "Throne Room of the Gods." Optionally, visit Broad Peak Base Camp or other nearby sites.

Day-13 :Concordia to Ali Camp +

Begin your journey towards Gondogoro La Pass, setting up camp at Ali Camp amid rugged mountain scenery.

Day-14 : Ali Camp to Gondogoro La Pass and Khuspang +

Start early for the challenging ascent of Gondogoro La Pass (5,940m). Enjoy breathtaking views of the Karakoram range before descending to Khuspang for your final night in the wilderness.

Day-15 : Khuspang to Hushe +

Continue your descent to Hushe Valley. Drive to the charming village of Hushe, where you’ll camp and interact with the welcoming locals.

Day-16 : Hushe to Skardu +

Travel through the picturesque Shyok and Indus river valleys back to Skardu. Enjoy a comfortable stay in your hotel with hot showers and relaxation.

Day-17 : Explore Skardu +

Discover the beauty and culture of Skardu, exploring local attractions and enjoying the city’s unique charm

Day-18 : Fly to Islamabad +

On this day, participants will fly from Skardu to Islamabad, enjoying time to relax at the hotel upon arrival.

Day19 : Contingency day for flight cancellation +

This day is reserved for any potential delays if the domestic flight didn't operate the previous day. Guests will depart early from Chilas to Islamabad, a journey that can take up to ten hours. However, if participants arrive in Islamabad on the domestic flight, it will be a free day for sightseeing in the city.

Day-20 : Fly to home country +

Participants will fly back to their home country today. Safe travels!
