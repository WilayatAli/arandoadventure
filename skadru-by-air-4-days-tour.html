4-Day By-Air Tour: Discover the Splendor of Skardu

Description

Embark on an extraordinary journey to Skardu Valley, where the richness of culture and the magnificence of nature come together to create an unforgettable experience. This tour offers an immersive exploration of Pakistan’s cultural and natural treasures, beginning with the ancient Skardu Fort, a testament to the region’s storied past, and continuing through the vibrant Shigar Bazaar, where traditional crafts and local life vividly unfold. The Amburiq Mosque, dating back to 1370, showcases exquisite wooden craftsmanship and spiritual serenity. Experience the warmth of Balti hospitality, savor traditional cuisine, and engage with timeless rituals and music that reflect the region's rich heritage. Skardu Valley itself is a masterpiece of natural beauty, featuring the tranquil Lower Kachura Lake and the lush landscapes of Shigar Valley, all framed by the breathtaking expanses of Deosai National Park. Here, every view reveals towering mountains, crystal-clear rivers, and a panorama that captivates the soul.

HightLights

  • Discover the ancient Skardu Fort and delve into the region’s rich history. Stroll through Shigar Bazaar, a vibrant marketplace showcasing local culture.

  • Admire the architectural beauty of the Amburiq Mosque.

  • Savor traditional Balti cuisine and experience the region’s warm hospitality.

  • Marvel at Lower Kachura Lake and Shigar Valley’s lush landscapes. Explore Deosai National Park, a haven for wildlife enthusiasts.

Travel Plans

1
Day 1: Arrival in Skardu

Upon arriving in Skardu via a scenic flight from Islamabad with PIA or Air Blue, you’ll be greeted by our guide at the airport. Begin your exploration at Shangrila Resort, often referred to as "Heaven on Earth," and capture its beauty through photography. Explore the serene Lower Kachura Lake, followed by a boat ride on Upper Kachura Lake. Visit the picturesque Xhoq Valley, and then head towards Skardu city. Enjoy dinner at your hotel and settle in for an overnight stay.

2
Day 2: Khaplu Valley and Manthokha Waterfall

Embark on a journey to the enchanting Khaplu Valley, known for its lush landscapes. Visit the historic Khaplu Fort, which stands as a testament to the region's rich history. Explore the ancient Chaqchan Mosque, dating back to 1370, reputedly built by Mir Sayyid Ali Hamadani. Discover the awe-inspiring Manthokha Waterfall, a hidden gem nestled in Skardu. Along the way, experience the beauty of Kharmang Valley. Return to Skardu by evening, enjoy dinner at your hotel, and spend the night at your accommodation.

3
Day 3: Shigar Valley Exploration

Begin your day with a drive from Skardu to Shigar Valley, soaking in the panoramic views along the way. Visit the historic Shigar Fort, built in the 17th century by the Raja of the Amacha Dynasty, and delve into its rich history. Wander through local markets to experience the essence of daily life. Visit the Amburiq Mosque, renowned for its beautifully carved wooden structure. Enjoy a traditional Balti lunch at a local restaurant, and then visit Blind Lake. Proceed to the Sarfaranga Cold Desert for a jeep safari and optional paragliding experience. Return to Skardu, enjoy dinner at your hotel, and stay overnight.

4
Day 4: Return to Islamabad

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and head to the airport. Fly back to Islamabad, cherishing the memories of an unforgettable expedition through the captivating landscapes and rich culture of Skardu Valley.
