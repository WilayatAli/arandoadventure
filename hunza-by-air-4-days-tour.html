4 Days By-Air Tour: Experience the Wonders of Hunza

Description

Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of Hunza Valley with this 4-day air tour. This itinerary is designed for travelers looking to explore the stunning landscapes and rich culture of Hunza efficiently and comfortably. Enjoy panoramic views, historical sites, and local experiences without the long overland journey.

HightLights

  • Scenic Flight to Hunza: Enjoy a breathtaking flight to Hunza, offering unparalleled views of the Karakoram Range.

  • Hunza Valley Exploration: Discover the charm of Karimabad, the historic Baltit and Altit Forts, and the picturesque Attabad Lake.

  • Passu Cones and Hussaini Suspension Bridge: Experience the striking Passu Cones and the thrilling Hussaini Suspension Bridge.

  • Local Culture and Cuisine: Engage with the friendly locals and savor traditional Hunza dishes.

Travel Plans

1
Day 1: Arrival and Journey to Hunza

Upon landing at Gilgit Airport, you will have a brief stop for refreshment before starting your drive toward the enchanting Hunza Valley. As you travel, you'll pause at the Rakaposhi Viewpoint to marvel at the breathtaking vistas of Rakaposhi Peak. Continuing your journey, you'll traverse through the scenic Nagar Valley, taking in the stunning landscapes that frame your route. By evening, you will arrive in Hunza, where you'll check into your hotel. After settling in, enjoy a delightful dinner at the hotel and unwind for the night.

2
Day 2: Attabad Lake and Passu Cones

Start your day with a hearty breakfast at the hotel before setting out for Attabad Lake. This lake, with its striking turquoise waters, promises a visual feast. Enjoy a serene boat ride on the lake, taking in the natural beauty that surrounds you. Afterward, you'll drive to the iconic Passu Cones, known for their distinctive pointed peaks. Capture the unique landscape with your camera and then visit the Hussaini Suspension Bridge, a thrilling experience offering panoramic views. By evening, you’ll head back to Hunza, where you'll have dinner at the hotel and relax for the night.

3
Day 3: Exploration of Hunza

Begin your day with breakfast at a local café in Karimabad, the heart of Hunza Valley. Your exploration starts with a visit to Baltit Fort, an ancient structure that provides panoramic views of the Hunza Valley and insights into the region’s rich history. Following your visit, enjoy a lunch featuring local Hunza cuisine, including Chapshuro, momos, and apricot-based dishes. In the afternoon, make your way to Eagle’s Nest, where you can admire the breathtaking views of peaks like Ultar Sar, Ladyfinger Peak, and Hunza Peak. Return to Karimabad for a delicious dinner at a local restaurant before heading back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

4
Day 4: Return to Islamabad

After breakfast, you will depart from Hunza and drive back to Gilgit. Upon arrival at Gilgit Airport, you'll catch your flight back to Islamabad via PIA. Your journey concludes as you arrive in Islamabad, marking the end of a memorable 4-day tour of Hunza, filled with unforgettable experiences and stunning landscapes.

Package Includes

  • Airfare: Round-trip airfare between Islamabad and Gilgit.

  • Accommodation: Stay in standard hotels with daily breakfast included.

  • Meals: Three meals a day (breakfast, lunch, and dinner). Note: Lunch is optional depending on the specific package.

  • Airport Transfers: Pick-up and drop-off at Gilgit Airport.

  • Ground Transportation: Transportation within Hunza for sightseeing.

  • Guide Services: Professional tour guide services for local excursions.

  • Taxes and Fees: All applicable taxes and airport fees

Package Exclusions

  • Entrance Fees: Costs for entrance to forts and other attractions.

  • Optional Activities: Fees for optional activities and any personal expenses.

  • Travel Insurance: Travel insurance is not included.

  • Tips and Gratuities: Tips for guides, drivers, and other service personnel are not included.
